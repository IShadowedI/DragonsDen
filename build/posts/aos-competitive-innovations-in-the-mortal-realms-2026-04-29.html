[AoS] Competitive Innovations in the Mortal Realms: 2026-04-29

April 29th, 2026 via Goonhammer
Welcome to Competitive Innovations, our weekly feature for a number of game lines across the tabletop wargame space. Each week our army list and tactics experts canvass the top tournaments for their game of specialty, looking at the week’s top match-ups and how they played out on the tabletop to let you know what’s hot, what’s not, and what might be coming next as the competitive meta evolves year-round.

This week we're looking at the first Warhammer Open outside of the US, in the Netherlands. It had a pretty solid turnout, with over a hundred players vying for a Golden Ticket. With so many new books to break in, there's a lot of room here to see how the meta is shaping up for those armies. Specifically, we'll see a lot of Sylvaneth and Daughters of Khaine.

We also have some smaller events going on, two in the US and one in Sweden.

This Week's Events

  • Age of Sigmar: Warhammer Open Maastricht - Netherlands
  • Age of Sigmar Warpstone Wars GT 2026 - United States
  • Iron Brawl 2026 Age of Sigmar - United States
  • Fantasia Fanatic XLIX Age of Sigmar - Sweden

Thoughts

The new Sylvaneth book brings a lot of fun teleportation shenanigans to the table, and this list leans on a lot of recursion to go against the hordes that FEC have. This is in heavy contrast to the hyper-elite lists that Sylvaneth had been running up this point.

This gives Sylvaneth more bodies to screen out threats and as the Overgrown mechanic takes over the board, will bog the FEC down with debuffs (and buffs to the Sylvaneth). While the FEC have far more bodies to throw at the problem, a skilled Sylvaneth player (Able to make it this far at 6-0) could outmaneuver them quite deftly by playing keep away a while and infecting terrain.

Archetype

Kurnoths and support

Thoughts

It really is refreshing to not have to just type Sylvaneth elite or Sylvaneth big boys again like I've done almost every week for the past year and a half. We're seeing the new book in action and it looks like it really shook things up.

Kurnoth are still some of the best melee fighters in the army, and tanky to boot. Their ability to reduce rend when near overgrown terrain hides a stronger resilience to damage than their 4+ Ward would imply. Although Belthanos no longer grants free run and charge, he does give them improvement movement and a teleport, allowing them to set up in an advantageous position to anticipate a charge, rather than hunt down their prey. Drycha is a gnarly beat stick that can also teleport into combat with anything that foolishly drifts too close to overgrown terrain. The Warsong Revenants bring it all together, giving the army its first priest, a ward and a penalty to enemy wards, making all of these units very dangerous to fight.

Most of the chaff here can be regenerated at half strength, making them great for screening out threats and stealing objectives.

Thoughts

This Ossiarch list is mostly meant to deal with more elite lists. The Kavalos Deathrider trick (charge with Liege, do mortals and leave) allows you to ping off of scarier threats and not worry about the counter attack lest they be pulled away from something more important. Doesn't work quite as good against more horde-heavy lists like DOK. Having more units means more board coverage, which makes it harder to run to safety.

Archetype

Horde DOK

Thoughts

This is a neat little list. It forsakes any of the big pieces, aside from a Bloodwrack Shrine, in order to cram in as many Blood Sisters as it can. Blood Sisters are gnarly, with Crit Mortals and Strike Last against smaller wound units, they can be hard to deal with if you don't hit them first with a high wound unit.

The Blood Hags help get the momentum going early, killing a chaff unit to get some early Blood Rites, while the priests and wizards hang back and buff the Blood Sisters. Finally you got some Heartrenders and Khinerai to help grab objectives outside of the murder zone.

Thoughts

This is a wild match up. Both armies are fast and strong, have chaff to protect them and with an extremely tanky piece behind it all.

A huge boon for the FEC here is the Beastflayers, which have a huge advantage against Morathi. Simply shutting off her rampage is a big way of making her easier to deal with, not to mention the damage reduction. Accompanied by something that can hit a little harder, like a Zombie Dragon or Ushoran himself taking her down in melee becomes a lot more probable.

Archetype

Ushoran and Friends

Thoughts

With the Terrorgheist lists effectively dead, FEC is going back to basics and hey, it still works. Ushoran leads the army both for his ability to cast spells, manipulate the delusion dice and just hit like an absolute truck. His hordes of minions serve to both protect the key pieces of the army and hold objectives.

Supporting him are the Zombie Dragon and Decapitator, both pretty strong hammers in their own right as long as they can get to swing first, so use the hordes to blunt the enemy advance and then swoop in and clear them out.

Thoughts

This is a real nailbiter of a game. Both armies are glass cannons and can get absolutely decimated if they're hit at the wrong time (particularly Sylvaneth, who want to make sure they're not outside of their terrain safety bubble). Both armies are playing semi-elite, with some real big named heroes and then some mid range and minimal chaff. This means that the bigger pieces are going to come to blows sooner rather than later and it's largely going to come down to who can make the most opportune charge rolls first.

Archetype

Morathi and Krethusa

Thoughts

With the new book, Morathi and Krethusa buried the hatchet and play a lot better together. Their buffs no longer confined to one side of the book, they can be run in any list you want and while expensive, make an excellent pairing. Morathi is the best Wizard in the book (accompanied by a very powerful hammer), while Krethusa is the best Priest.

The main troops of the army are the Blood Sisters, which inflict Crit mortals and Strike Last, while sisters of slaughter help screen out the enemy and Khinerai and run and grab objectives.
