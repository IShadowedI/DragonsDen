Explore The ‘Northlands’, Get Weird With ‘Hellboy’, And More With These 5E Adventures

Meaghan Colleran
5 Minute Read
May 1 2026

It’s time to roll the dice on new releases! Come check out this week’s batch of brand-new 5e compatible tabletop adventures and supplements.

Sol’Kesh: The Speculative Evolution Bestiary

This is a nearly 300-page campaign setting, set on a primal island of weird evolution paths and weirder D&D creatures. Ninety-eight new potential friends are in there, many of whom can be raised from eggs, tamed, and kept as pets and mounts. Forage, explore, and discover everything this brand-new fantasy biome has, from natural traps to unique weather systems, to creatures to bully your DM into letting you keep.

Isolated for eons, Sol’Kesh is an island without humanity, without a species to spark flame and conquer. A primal landscape of predator and prey, clawing at the edge of extinction, where a single law of life reigns supreme, evolve or die.

Competition breeds adaptation and an immense variety of life has taken root and flourished. From miles of exposed coral reefs, sunken forests and fungi-lit caves that riddle the earth below. Wild and unique flora and fauna struggle in an evolutionary arms race, fighting for their next breath, their next meal and for their next generation to survive.

Northlands Worldbook

Travel to the Northlands. Do battle with heroes and gods, slay dragons, and seek your glory. This setting introduces new subclasses, monsters, spells, items, weapons, and endless opportunities for adventure.

Northlands Worldbook is a campaign overlay for D&D 2024. Roam the mountainous halls of the jotun, slay new dragons, repel bloodthirsty raiders to gain glory and fame, and wield new magic against the dangers of the North.

• Play 8 new subclasses, including the Nornbound Cleric or Trickster Warlock, and 5 new species, including giantkin and bearfolk.
• Unleash new 50 monsters, 9 new factions, and over 80 new weapons, spells, and magic items.
• Adventure into fully realized drop-in locations, including the Realm of Bears, the Kingdoms of the Giants, and more.
• Sail to new lands or win fame with new rules for sailing, ship combat, rune magic, fate, and earning a reputation.

The Lazy DM’s Companion

We’ve all been the lazy DM. Sometimes we all need a little boost to get our next adventure rolling. And this book gives us everything from tools for improvisation, session zero checklists, and tips for everything from one-on-one to combat, to creating clues.

The Lazy DM’s Companion is a 64 page book of guidelines and inspirational generators to help you build, prepare, and run awesome D&D games. Sitting alongside Return of the Lazy Dungeon Master and The Lazy DM’s Workbook, the Lazy DM’s Companion streamlines play and fires off your imagination to build awesome and unique worlds for you and your players to explore.

Each page of this 64-page book offers useful guidelines, an adventure or campaign generator, or a versatile map within which to build your own adventures.

Hellboy: Punch Nazis and Other Strangeness

Join BPRD, find some of the world’s weirdest little-known creatures, and, of course, punch a Nazi. This book brings the universe and adventure of Hellboy to your 5E campaign. So grab your case file and start looking into the source of all of that new weirdness.

Within this book, you’ll find vampires and werewolves, faerie curses and haunted houses, the vengeful dead and the manaical living, spiderwomen, goatmen, frog monsters, wise naga, the maddest Nazi “science,” and an ITIL-compliant scheme to steal your very soul! The time has come…to Punch Nazis!

This supplement includes
– 13 Case Files for your B.P.R.D. Agents.
– A variety of bonus Agents (living and dead) to expand your NPC roster.

D&D Rules Cyclopedia

Playing D&D can involve a lot of different books and supplements. But this comprehensive Cyclopedia puts it all in one easy-to-reference place. Whether you’re a DM or a player from level one through whatever your table maxes at, this book will have helpful information for your quick referencing needs.

Whether you’re a player or a Dungeon Master, the Dungeons & Dragons Rules Cyclopedia is now the comprehensive sourcebook you need for the original fantasy roleplaying game! For ages 12 and up, the Cyclopedia contains the compete game system and hundreds of features, including the following:

– All the rules from the D&D boxed set series, including Basic, Expert, Companion, and Masters
– Guidelines to develop and play characters from levels 1-36
– Comprehensive lists of weaponry and equipment
– Expansion rules including optional skills and talents
– An overview of the Known World and the HOLLOW WORLD game setting, the official D&D campaign world; and Rules to convert D&D games and characters into AD&D 2nd edition game statistics and back again

Fully compatible with the new DUNGEONS & DRAGONS Boxed Set, this volume allows players the full scope of fantasy role-playing from dungeons to the Outer Planes. Now, more than ever, the DUNGEON & DRAGONS game is ready and waiting for you.

The Warlock’s Spell Compendium

This in-universe text book provides a quick and immersive reference for and unique take on Warlock spells. Why should wizards have all of the fun collecting books and spells? Now your character can also lug around a collection of all of their knowledge, and that’s sure to make their patron happy.

Have you ever wanted your own spell book? Using a modified version of my best selling book “the Spell Writing Guide” and the “Spell Saying Guide” you can now write all your warlock spells in a way guaranteed to please your patron, containing all base warlock spells in 5e. Containing the explanation of my new system (aspect-based spell writing), details on its use for deriving new damage types, and evolving spells, this book contains significantly more science than previous efforts so even if you are just a curious wizard, this may be required reading for your studies.

Presented as an in universe textbook given to students at the University of Theoretical Magic, this book should prove a fun reference for both roleplaying and personal character prop making. I hope you enjoy!
