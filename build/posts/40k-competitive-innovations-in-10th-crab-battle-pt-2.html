Standard Ultramarines business here, with tanks and men that all slap hard and take that bit too much effort to kill. The Marines are suffering a bit in Defiler land, but are still very potent and more than capable of pushing through the top armies on their day. Great work on the top placing.

The Best of the Rest

There were 4 more players on 4-1 records. They were:
  • 3rd - Austin Warfel - Orks (Da Big Hunt): Massed Beastsnaggas and Kill Rigs in this rarely seen Big Hunt list.
  • 4th - Kyle Myers - Deathwatch (Black Spear Task Force): Indomitor / Fortis Kill Teams.
  • 5th - Nolan Haley - World Eaters (Khorne Daemonkin): A VERY Intriguing Khorne list with loads of Bloodletters and double Defilers.
  • 6th - Paul Muzila - Adepta Sororitas (Hallowed Martyrs): Go-wide character and infantry spam.

Wrap Up

Don't get Crabby, castle with Abby.

Lowest of Men, out.
